RSVP Button Concepts

Click each button to see the satisfying press animation

Neon Race Track

A glowing light races around the border continuously. Pops with energy when clicked.

Gradient River

Smooth flowing gradient border. Ripple effect expands from click point.

Bouncy Blob

Soft pulsing glow at rest. Squishes and bounces like jelly when pressed.

Sparkle Burst

Shimmering light sweeps across. Sparkles explode outward on click.

Elastic Press

Arcade-style 3D button. Physically depresses and bounces back up.

Double Orbit

Glowing orbs orbit the button continuously. Pulses brightly on click.

Confetti Explosion

Subtle shimmer at rest. Colorful confetti bursts out when clicked.

Liquid Fill

Fills from bottom on hover. Wobbles like liquid when selected.
